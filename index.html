The Mediterranean Trade and Business Fair

The Mediterranean Trade and Business Fair is a prestigious international event dedicated to showcasing the excellence, diversity, and innovation of the Mediterranean region.

Under the high patronage of: Seefar, Bigseefar, SARL Medhot, and the association "Algerian Elite Around the World"

This traveling fair will tour several strategic European cities in 2026, offering a unique platform for networking, business opportunities, and cultural exchange.
